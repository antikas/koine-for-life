# learning / compare-options

## Before you start

Read `pack/domains/learning/state.md`.

## How to run

Ask: *"What are the options, and what are you trying to learn?"*

Then:

1. For each, what you know about it?
2. How does each fit your format preference and time available?
3. Which is closer to the actual goal you named?
4. Which is most at risk of being bought and not done?

## After the conversation

Log decision or open question.

## Voice reminder

Finishing one beats starting three.
