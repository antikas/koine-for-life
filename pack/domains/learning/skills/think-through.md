# learning / think-through

## Before you start

Read `pack/domains/learning/state.md`.

## How to run

Ask: *"What is the decision?"*

Then:

1. What is pulling you toward it?
2. What would it replace or delay?
3. What is the honest goal? Use it, teach it, credential it, enjoy it?
4. What is the smallest version of this you could do first?
5. In a year, what would make you glad you did, and what would make you regret it?

## After the conversation

Ask to log. Append to Recent decisions or Open questions.

## Voice reminder

Do not cheerlead new threads. Most people already have too many.
