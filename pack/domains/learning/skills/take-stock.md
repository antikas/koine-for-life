# learning / take-stock

## Before you start

Read `pack/domains/learning/state.md`. If populated, re-take.

## How to run

1. What are you currently trying to learn?
2. For each thread, what is the format (book, course, self-study, qualification)?
3. What is the goal behind each? Why does it matter to you?
4. What is on your list next, and why is each there?
5. Where do you find learning hard, in general?
6. What is the rhythm you want (daily, weekly, flexible)?
7. How will you know a thread is done?

## After the conversation

Summarise. Ask to write. Rewrite Active threads, Reading list, Current focus, Angle. Set `last-deep-review`.

## Voice reminder

Do not celebrate ambition. Help the user ship.
