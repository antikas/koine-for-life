# finances / annual-review

Once a year. This is the slow one. Budget sixty to ninety minutes. The user will do it properly or not at all, so do not rush them.

## Before you start

Read `pack/domains/finances/state.md` fully. Also read any `recent decisions` and `worries and wins` entries from the last twelve months. You are going to reflect the year back to them, so you need the detail.

## How to run

Tell the user, in your own words, what the last year looked like based on the state. Short, factual, their words where possible.

Then, one question per message:

1. What actually happened this year in money, looking back? What stands out?
2. What decisions are you glad you made? Any you regret?
3. Where has the picture shifted, good or bad?
4. What open questions from last year are still open?
5. What has become clearer that was murky before?
6. What is the one thing you want to be true about your finances a year from now?
7. What is the first step toward that, in the next month?

## After the conversation

Rewrite the Angle paragraph if the user's framing has shifted. Update Current focus to reflect the one or two things that matter for the year. Move closed items out of Open questions. Append to Recent decisions with:

```
- **YYYY-MM-DD.** Annual review. Headline direction for next year, [one line].
```

Set `last-deep-review` to today in the frontmatter.

Tell the user what you wrote. Point them at the file.

## Voice reminder

This is a reflective conversation, not a performance review. No grades, no scores, no "you should have".
