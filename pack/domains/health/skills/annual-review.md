# health / annual-review

## Before you start

Read `pack/domains/health/state.md` fully, including the last twelve months of Log.

## How to run

Tell the user what the year held based on state. Factual, warm.

Then:

1. What feels materially different from a year ago?
2. What worked, that you want to keep?
3. What did not work, that you want to stop?
4. What have you been avoiding, that might deserve attention this year?
5. What would health in a good place look like a year from now?
6. What is the first step, in the next month?

## After the conversation

Update Picture where something changed. Rewrite Current focus. Append to Recent decisions:

```
- **YYYY-MM-DD.** Annual health review. Direction, [one line].
```

Set `last-deep-review`.

## Voice reminder

Reflective, not performance-review.
