# health / compare-options

## Before you start

Read `pack/domains/health/state.md`.

## How to run

Ask: *"What are the options, and what decision are they for?"*

Then:

1. For each, what do you know?
2. What do you still not know?
3. What matters most here (time, cost, fit, intensity, reputation)?
4. Which option matches your picture best?

For clinical options (treatments, medications), push toward a clinician conversation.

## After the conversation

Lay back short. Log decision or open question.

## Voice reminder

Stay out of clinical territory.
