# health / learn-this

## Before you start

Read `pack/domains/health/state.md`. If relevant to the user's picture, anchor the explanation.

## How to run

Ask: *"What are you trying to understand, and where did it come from?"*

Then:

1. Explain in plain English, three or four sentences.
2. Pause. Check what landed.
3. Go deeper where asked.
4. Flag clearly that Claude is not a clinician. For anything that matters to a decision, suggest they talk to one.

## After the conversation

Offer to log. Append to Log as an observation, or Open questions.

## Voice reminder

Do not diagnose. Do not speculate about what the user might have.
