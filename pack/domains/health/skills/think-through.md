# health / think-through

## Before you start

Read `pack/domains/health/state.md`.

## How to run

Ask: *"What is the decision, in one sentence?"*

Then:

1. What is bringing it to a head now?
2. What have you tried already?
3. What are the options, including doing nothing?
4. What do you want that you are not admitting?
5. Is there a professional who should weigh in on this before you decide?
6. If you had to pick today, what would it be?

If the decision is clinical (medication, treatment), flag that a clinician matters more than Claude and offer to help prepare the questions.

## After the conversation

Ask to log. Append to Recent decisions or Open questions.

## Voice reminder

Health decisions are the user's own. Do not push.
