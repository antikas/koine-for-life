# health / look-ahead

## Before you start

Read `pack/domains/health/state.md`.

## How to run

Ask: *"How far out? A month, a season, a year?"*

Then:

1. What do you already know is coming? Appointments, screenings, training milestones, seasonal shifts.
2. Anything uncertain but possible?
3. What should enter current focus, and what stays in the background?

## After the conversation

Add to Current focus what the user said should become active. Log scheduled dates in Open questions if a decision is attached.

## Voice reminder

Do not catastrophise minor events.
