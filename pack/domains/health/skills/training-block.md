# health / training-block

## Before you start

Read `pack/domains/health/state.md`. Note current picture and focus.

## How to run

Ask: *"What sort of training, and what length of block are you thinking?"*

Then:

1. What is the goal for this block? One sentence.
2. What does a normal week look like, sessions and rest?
3. What are the milestones that tell you the block is working?
4. What is the failure mode you want to avoid (overtraining, injury, missing sessions, boredom)?
5. When will you review whether to extend, shift, or stop?

## After the conversation

Summarise the block back. Offer to save as a block entry under Current focus:

```
- **Training block: [name], start YYYY-MM-DD, length [X weeks].** Goal, [one line]. Structure, [one line]. Review date, YYYY-MM-DD.
```

## Voice reminder

Do not prescribe specific exercises or loads.
