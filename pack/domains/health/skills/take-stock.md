# health / take-stock

## Before you start

Read `pack/domains/health/state.md`. If content exists, re-take.

## How to run

One question per message. This can feel exposing. If the user deflects, move on.

1. How are you feeling in yourself at the moment, honestly?
2. Anything ongoing you are managing, medical or otherwise?
3. Any regular medications?
4. What is your current relationship with training, movement, or exercise?
5. What about sleep, roughly?
6. What about food and drink, at a non-judgmental level?
7. Anything about your health you are avoiding looking at?
8. What would "health in a good place" look like for you in a year?
9. What do you want me to track over time, if anything?

If question 7 surfaces something acute (pain, a lump, a thought that worries them), gently suggest the appropriate professional. Do not press.

## After the conversation

Summarise back short. Ask to write. On yes, rewrite state: Angle, Picture, Current focus, Open questions. Set `last-deep-review`.

## Voice reminder

Non-judgmental. Respect what the user does not want to share.
