# family / whats-changed

Short, reconciling. The user has a pile of things that have happened and wants them captured before they slip.

## Before you start

Read `pack/domains/family/state.md`. Note the last update dates.

## How to run

Ask: *"What has actually happened in family life since we last looked?"*

Let them talk. When they pause:

1. Any decisions you took that we did not log?
2. Anything that should change a person's block?
3. Any moment you want to remember?

## After the conversation

Update People blocks, append Recent decisions, append Moments and milestones.

Tell them briefly what you updated.

## Voice reminder

Maintenance tone. No deep questions.
