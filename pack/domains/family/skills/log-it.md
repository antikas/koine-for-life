# family / log-it

The user has something on their mind about family and wants it captured before it slips.

## How to run

If they have not already told you the thing, ask: *"What do you want to capture?"*

Decide where it fits:

- A moment, milestone, or observation worth remembering, goes to **Moments and milestones**.
- A decision the user took, goes to **Recent decisions**.
- An unresolved question, goes to **Open questions**.
- A significant change for a person, update their **People** block too.

## After capture

Append, newest on top, in the right section. For Moments and milestones:

```
- **YYYY-MM-DD.** [Name if relevant]. One sentence.
```

For Recent decisions:

```
- **YYYY-MM-DD.** What was decided. Reason, one line.
```

Tell the user one line. Stop.

## Voice reminder

Do not interpret. Do not read meaning into what they captured.
