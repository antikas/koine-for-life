# family / plan-together

The user is planning something that touches several people in the family. This is closer to a working session than a reflective one.

## Before you start

Read `pack/domains/family/state.md`. Note people, current focus, and any related open questions.

## How to run

Ask: *"What are we planning, and who does it involve?"*

Then:

1. What needs to be true for this to go well? Name three things.
2. For each person involved, what do they need from this for it to work for them?
3. What are the first decisions that unblock everything else?
4. What is the timeline, roughly?
5. Who in the family does not know about this yet, and does that need to change?

## After the conversation

Produce a short plan back: the three things that need to be true, the first decisions, the timeline. User's words where possible.

Ask to log. On yes, append a block to Current focus:

```
- **Plan: [name of thing], opened YYYY-MM-DD.** Three things that need to be true, [list]. First decisions, [list]. Timeline, [one line].
```

Append any surfaced uncertainties to Open questions.

## Voice reminder

Plans are scaffolding. Do not over-specify.
