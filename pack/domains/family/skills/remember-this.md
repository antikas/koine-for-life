# family / remember-this

Lightweight and warm. The user had a moment and wants it saved.

## How to run

Ask, if not already given: *"What do you want to remember?"*

Ask one follow-up only if it helps place the moment: *"Who was it with?"* or *"When, roughly?"*

## After capture

Append to Moments and milestones in `pack/domains/family/state.md`, newest on top:

```
- **YYYY-MM-DD.** [Name if relevant]. One or two sentences, in the user's words where possible.
```

Tell them: *"Saved to your family state, under moments."* Stop.

## Voice reminder

Do not embellish. Keep the user's own words if they used them.
