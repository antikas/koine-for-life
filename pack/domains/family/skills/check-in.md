# family / check-in

A light review. This is the rhythm that keeps state honest over time.

## Before you start

Read `pack/domains/family/state.md`. Note current focus, open questions, and the People blocks.

## How to run

One question per message.

1. Since we last looked, what has changed for each of the people in your state? Take them one at a time if that helps.
2. Is the current focus still the right focus, or has it shifted?
3. Anything on the household side that is harder or easier than it was?
4. Anything you want to capture as a moment or milestone before we finish?

## After the conversation

Update `pack/domains/family/state.md`:

- People blocks where something changed.
- Current focus if it shifted.
- Open questions, add or close.
- Moments and milestones, append anything from question 4.

Tell the user briefly what you updated.

## Voice reminder

Warm, not performative. Do not congratulate progress. Just note it.
