# family / take-stock

Run once the user is ready to go deeper than the opening interview. Again after a major life change (new child, loss, move, separation).

## Before you start

Read `pack/domains/family/state.md`. If it has real content, this is a re-take. Say what is there and ask what has changed.

## How to run

One question per message. If a topic is tender, slow down or skip. The user's pace wins.

1. Tell me who is in your family, in the widest sense you want to use that word.
2. Starting with [partner if any], what is going on for them right now?
3. For each child, tell me about them. Age, what they love, what they find hard.
4. Who else is close enough that they should be in the picture?
5. What is the rhythm of a normal week in your household?
6. What is the best thing about family life right now?
7. What is the hardest thing?
8. Is there anything on the horizon that is going to change family life in the next year?
9. What do you want me to remember about each person, so I do not have to ask again?

## After the conversation

Summarise back short. Ask to write.

On yes, rewrite `pack/domains/family/state.md`. Set `last-deep-review` to today. Fill Angle, People (one block per person), Household, Current focus, Open questions. Leave Recent decisions and Moments and milestones as they were.

Name the file, say briefly what you wrote where.

## Voice reminder

Family is emotional ground. Reflect, do not diagnose. Use the names the user gave you.
