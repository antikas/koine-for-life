# family / look-ahead

The user wants to see the months ahead so nothing sneaks up.

## Before you start

Read `pack/domains/family/state.md`. Note Current focus and People blocks.

## How to run

Ask: *"How far out do you want to look? Next term, six months, a year?"*

Then:

1. What do you already know is coming for each person? School events, exams, birthdays, transitions, medical, trips.
2. Anything uncertain but possible? A visit, a move, a job change for your partner, a grandparent milestone.
3. Of those, which are you not quite thought through?
4. Which one should become part of current focus?

## After the conversation

Add to Current focus only what the user said should become active. Log the rest lightly in Open questions.

## Voice reminder

Do not catastrophise. Some things on the horizon are just things.
