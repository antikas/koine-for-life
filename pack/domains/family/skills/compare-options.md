# family / compare-options

The user has named options and wants to see them side by side.

## Before you start

Read `pack/domains/family/state.md`. Note which people the decision affects most.

## How to run

Ask: *"What are the options, and who in the family does this affect most?"*

Then:

1. For each option, what do you know already?
2. What do you still not know?
3. What is the criterion that matters most here (fit, cost, distance, stretch, stability)?
4. Against that criterion, how do they rank in your head?
5. Is there a person in the family whose preference should weigh more on this?

## After the conversation

Lay the comparison back short. Ask whether to log. On yes, append to Recent decisions if decided, Open questions if not.

## Voice reminder

Do not introduce options the user did not name unless asked.
