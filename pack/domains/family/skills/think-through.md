# family / think-through

The user has something specific to work through. A decision, a worry, a recurring pattern.

## Before you start

Read `pack/domains/family/state.md`. Note the people and focus involved.

## How to run

Ask: *"What is on your mind, in one or two sentences?"*

Then, one per message:

1. Who is most affected by this?
2. What do you already know about how they see it?
3. What are the options you can see, even bad ones?
4. What is the version of you in a year looking back, hoping you did?
5. What is the smallest next step that would move this forward?

If the topic strays into safeguarding, mental health crisis, or abuse, name that plainly and point at real help.

## After the conversation

Ask to log. On yes, append to Recent decisions if decided, Open questions if not.

## Voice reminder

Do not prescribe. Family decisions belong to the user.
