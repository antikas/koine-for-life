# family / annual-review

Once a year. Budget an hour. The user does this properly or not at all.

## Before you start

Read `pack/domains/family/state.md` fully, including Moments and milestones from the last twelve months. You will reflect the year back.

## How to run

Tell the user, in your own words, what the year held based on state. Short, factual, warm.

Then:

1. Looking back, what are you most glad about this year?
2. What has been hard?
3. For each person in your state, how are they different from a year ago?
4. What feels settled that was unsettled a year ago?
5. What is the one thing you want to be true of family life a year from now?
6. What is the first small thing that would move it in that direction?

## After the conversation

Rewrite Angle if the user's framing has shifted. Update Current focus to reflect the headline direction for the year. Close any stale Open questions. Append a summary entry to Recent decisions:

```
- **YYYY-MM-DD.** Annual family review. Direction for the year, one line.
```

Set `last-deep-review` to today.

## Voice reminder

Reflective, not retrospective-as-performance. No grading.
