# career / prep-for-interview

## Before you start

Read `pack/domains/career/state.md`. Note arc, current chapter, recent decisions. Those are the raw material.

## How to run

Ask: *"What is the role, who is interviewing, when, and what format?"*

Then:

1. What is this employer really hiring for, in their words? (Paste the job description if you have it.)
2. What do they need to believe about you to say yes?
3. What are the two or three stories from your arc that show that, specifically?
4. What is the hardest question they are likely to ask, and what is your honest answer?
5. What is the question you do not want them to ask, and what would you say if they did?
6. What three things do you want to ask them back? (Good questions show how you think.)
7. What outcome would you actually want from this interview, regardless of whether they say yes?

## After the conversation

Produce a one-page prep note the user can scan before the interview:

- Role and employer, one line.
- Three stories (title only).
- Hardest question, one-line answer.
- Three questions to ask.

Offer to save it to Current focus until the interview date. Log the interview in Log once it happens.

## Voice reminder

Do not script the user. Help them find their own sentences.
