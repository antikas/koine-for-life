# career / positioning

## Before you start

Read `pack/domains/career/state.md`. Arc and current chapter are the source material.

## How to run

Ask: *"What are you writing, and who is it for? CV, LinkedIn, a bio for a talk, a line in an email, an introduction in a meeting?"*

Then:

1. Who is the reader, and what do they need to know in one sentence to take you seriously?
2. What is the thread through your arc that matters here? Not everything, one thread.
3. What is the current chapter's verb? What are you doing right now, actively.
4. What is the specific, concrete proof that would make a stranger believe both?

Let the user draft. Offer one edit pass that strips flourish and keeps specifics. Two passes maximum.

## After the conversation

Offer to save the final version to Current chapter or Log:

```
- **YYYY-MM-DD, positioning.** Context, [CV/LinkedIn/bio]. Text, [one or two sentences].
```

## Voice reminder

Positioning sentences are usually shorter than the user's first draft. Strip, do not add.
