# career / stress-test

## Before you start

Read `pack/domains/career/state.md`. Also cross-reference `pack/domains/finances/state.md` if the scenario has financial weight, and note without prying into detail.

## How to run

Ask: *"What scenario do you want to look at?"*

Common ones: redundancy, the current role imploding, industry contraction, a health issue forcing time out, a merger or acquisition, the employer collapsing.

Then:

1. How likely is this, in your gut?
2. If it happened tomorrow, what goes wrong first?
3. How long before it is a financial problem?
4. What is your first move? The second?
5. Who is in your network who would matter?
6. Is there something cheap you could do now that would make the scenario easier if it came?

## After the conversation

Summarise plainly. Log in Recent decisions:

```
- **YYYY-MM-DD, stress-test.** Scenario, [one line]. Outcome, [one line]. Prep to do now, [one line or none].
```

If a prep action emerged, add to Current focus.

## Voice reminder

Do not reassure. The value is in clarity, not comfort.
