# career / compare-options

## Before you start

Read `pack/domains/career/state.md`.

## How to run

Ask: *"What are the options, and what decision are they for?"*

Then:

1. For each, what you know already. Work, team, comp, location, stretch, risk.
2. What do you still not know that you could find out?
3. What matters most here? Stretch, comp, stability, fit, story, time at home.
4. Against that criterion, how do the options rank in your head?
5. Which option, a year later, would you regret not taking?

## After the conversation

Lay the comparison back short. Ask to log. Append to Recent decisions or Open questions.

## Voice reminder

If one option is clearly worse, say so. Career comparisons fail when everything is "it depends".
