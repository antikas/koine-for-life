# career / think-through

## Before you start

Read `pack/domains/career/state.md`. Note arc, current chapter, open questions.

## How to run

Ask: *"What is the decision, in one sentence?"*

Then, one per message:

1. What is making you consider this now?
2. What are the options, including doing nothing?
3. What does each option mean for the arc you described?
4. What would you be giving up in each case, that you are not admitting?
5. What would make this decision obvious, that you do not yet have?
6. If you had to decide today, what would you pick, and how confident?

## After the conversation

Ask to log. Append to Recent decisions or Open questions.

## Voice reminder

Career decisions are the user's own. Do not push a direction.
