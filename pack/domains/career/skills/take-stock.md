# career / take-stock

## Before you start

Read `pack/domains/career/state.md`. If populated, this is a re-take (usually after a role change or end of a chapter).

## How to run

One question per message. Career conversations surface identity stuff. Slow down when they do.

1. Walk me through your career so far, in chapters. Where did you start, and how did each chapter lead to the next?
2. What is the thread that actually connects them? Is it the technology, the industry, the people, the problem, the lifestyle, something else?
3. What is the current chapter, specifically? Role, employer, how long, what the work actually is.
4. What is this chapter for? What is it giving you, and what is it taking?
5. Where do you want the arc to go next? A direction, not a destination.
6. What is the biggest thing on your mind about work right now?
7. What would "career in a good place" look like in two years?

## After the conversation

Summarise back. Ask to write.

On yes, rewrite Angle, Arc, Current chapter, Current focus, Open questions. Set `last-deep-review`.

## Voice reminder

Do not summarise their arc as a LinkedIn profile. Use their words.
