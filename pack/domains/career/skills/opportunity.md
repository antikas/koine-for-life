# career / opportunity

Claude does not act as a recruiter or evaluate offers commercially. Claude helps the user think about whether the opportunity is worth engaging with.

## Before you start

Read `pack/domains/career/state.md`. Note arc, current chapter, and any open questions around direction.

## How to run

Ask: *"Who approached you, about what, and how serious is the approach?"*

Then, one per message:

1. What is the role or thing, as you understand it?
2. Does it match the direction in your arc, or is it a pivot?
3. Why you, specifically, as far as you can tell?
4. What is the honest reason you are tempted? (Money, title, boredom, flattery, fit, freedom, all of the above.)
5. What would you need to find out before engaging further?
6. What is the next step, if any? A call, a formal application, a polite decline, a holding reply.

## After the conversation

Log the approach in Log:

```
- **YYYY-MM-DD, approach.** Source, [who]. About, [one line]. Status, [engaging / declined / parked].
```

If engaging, add a next-step to Current focus. If declined, note the reason briefly.

## Voice reminder

Do not hype the opportunity. Do not dismiss it. Reflect what the user is actually saying.
