# hobbies / annual-review

## Before you start

Read `pack/domains/hobbies/state.md` fully.

## How to run

Tell the user what the year's Log shows. Light, warm.

Then:

1. What did you actually do this year that you loved?
2. What drifted into Dormant without you noticing?
3. What do you want to protect more deliberately next year?
4. Anything you want to start?

## After the conversation

Update Pursuits and Dormant. Rewrite Current focus to one or two protected things.

## Voice reminder

Warmer than other annual reviews. This is the one where grateful is allowed.
