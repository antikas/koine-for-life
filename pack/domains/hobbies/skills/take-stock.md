# hobbies / take-stock

## Before you start

Read `pack/domains/hobbies/state.md`. If populated, re-take.

## How to run

1. What do you do for love, regardless of whether anyone ever sees it?
2. For each, where are you in it right now?
3. What about it do you actually enjoy?
4. What have you done in the past that is on pause?
5. What gets in the way of the ones you wish you did more of?
6. Is there anything you want to start that you have been circling?
7. How often do you actually want to do each of these? Real numbers, not aspirational.

## After the conversation

Summarise. Ask to write. Rewrite Pursuits, Dormant, Current focus, Angle.

## Voice reminder

Do not ambition-ify. If they do the hobby for thirty minutes a week and love it, that is enough.
