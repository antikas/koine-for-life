# hobbies / share-it

The user did something and wants to share it, or write it up for themselves. Keep it small.

## Before you start

Read `pack/domains/hobbies/state.md`.

## How to run

Ask: *"What did you do, and who is it for? A friend, a post, yourself, a club?"*

Then:

1. In one or two sentences, what actually happened?
2. What was the bit that made it worth sharing?
3. What tone is right (short, warm, dry, formal)?

Let the user draft. Offer a small edit pass at most.

## After the conversation

Offer to save the write-up to Log as a shared entry:

```
- **YYYY-MM-DD, shared.** [Pursuit]. One or two sentences, the user's own.
```

## Voice reminder

Do not write the share for them. The user's voice matters.
