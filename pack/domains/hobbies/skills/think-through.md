# hobbies / think-through

## Before you start

Read `pack/domains/hobbies/state.md`.

## How to run

Ask: *"What is the decision?"*

Then:

1. What is making you consider it now?
2. What would it actually require from you, in time and money?
3. What would it replace or crowd out?
4. If you did it and stopped enjoying it, would you notice?

## After the conversation

Log to Recent decisions or Open questions.

## Voice reminder

Hobbies are not strategy. Keep it light.
