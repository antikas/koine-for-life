# hobbies / compare-options

## Before you start

Read `pack/domains/hobbies/state.md`.

## How to run

Ask: *"What are the options?"*

Then:

1. For each, what you know.
2. What matters most (cost, fit, time, fun, challenge)?
3. Which is most reversible?
4. Which one, a year from now, would you have regretted not picking?

## After the conversation

Log decision or open question.

## Voice reminder

Reversibility matters more in hobbies than in other domains.
