# hobbies / fixtures

## Before you start

Read `pack/domains/hobbies/state.md`.

## How to run

Ask: *"Adding a fixture, reviewing past ones, or planning around one coming up?"*

### Adding

1. Which pursuit, what is the event, when?
2. Is it something you are in, or something you are attending?
3. What would make it a good experience, regardless of outcome?

### Reviewing

1. What was the fixture?
2. What did you take from it?

### Planning around

1. When is it, and what needs to be in shape by then?
2. What is the first step this week?

## After the conversation

For upcoming, append to Log or Current focus with date. For reviewed, append to Log.

## Voice reminder

Outcome is not the whole thing. Help the user notice what else was there.
