# ventures / think-through

## Before you start

Read `pack/domains/ventures/state.md`. Know the venture, its stage, and the current constraint.

## How to run

Ask: *"What is the decision, in one sentence, and which venture?"*

Then:

1. What is forcing a decision now?
2. What are the real options?
3. For each, what does it mean for the constraint we named in state?
4. What are you secretly hoping someone decides for you on?
5. What is the reversible vs irreversible part of this?
6. If you decide today, what would you choose, and how confident?

Push once on question 4. Ventures decisions often stall on this.

## After the conversation

Ask to log. Append to Recent decisions or Open questions.

## Voice reminder

Pushback is welcome here. Say what you actually think once asked. Still the user's decision.
