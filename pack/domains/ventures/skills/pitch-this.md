# ventures / pitch-this

## Before you start

Read `pack/domains/ventures/state.md`. Know the venture.

## How to run

Ask: *"Who are you pitching to, and in what format? Two-minute verbal, a page, a deck?"*

Then:

1. What problem are you solving, in one sentence a non-expert would understand?
2. Who has this problem badly enough to pay?
3. What is your answer, in one sentence?
4. Why is this the right team to do it?
5. What would you want someone to remember, if they only remembered one thing?

Ask the user to say the pitch out loud, rough. Then reflect back where it landed and where it wandered.

If they want to iterate, go again. Maximum three passes, then stop.

## After the conversation

Offer to save the sharpened pitch to the venture's block under a small "current pitch" subheading, dated.

## Voice reminder

The user does the pitch, not you. You are the mirror.
