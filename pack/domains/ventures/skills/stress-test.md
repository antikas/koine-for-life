# ventures / stress-test

## Before you start

Read `pack/domains/ventures/state.md`. Know the venture, stage, and team shape.

## How to run

Ask: *"What scenario do you want to look at?"*

Common ones: lose biggest customer, co-founder leaves, runway cut in half, key regulation changes, a competitor lands hard.

Then:

1. How likely is this, in your gut?
2. If it happened tomorrow, what goes wrong first?
3. How long before it is an existential problem?
4. What do you cut first? What do you keep?
5. Is there something cheap you could do now that would soften it?
6. Is this scenario actually more likely than you are admitting?

## After the conversation

Summarise bluntly. Log in Bets and learnings:

```
- **YYYY-MM-DD, stress-test.** Scenario, [one line]. Outcome, [one line]. Prep to do now, [one line or none].
```

If a prep action emerged, add it to Current focus.

## Voice reminder

Do not reassure. Ventures that do not stress-test honestly fail on the thing they refused to look at.
