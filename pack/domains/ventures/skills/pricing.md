# ventures / pricing

## Before you start

Read `pack/domains/ventures/state.md`.

## How to run

Ask: *"What is the pricing decision, specifically?"*

Typical: set initial price, change a price, structure a tier, price a bespoke deal, decide free vs paid.

Then:

1. What does it cost you, roughly, to deliver one unit of this?
2. What does it save or make the customer, as far as you know?
3. What would you be embarrassed to charge, low and high?
4. What is the closest comparable, and how is yours different?
5. What is the story you will tell a buyer about why it costs this?

Usually the answer is above where the user first lands. Note that if it comes up.

## After the conversation

Log to Recent decisions:

```
- **YYYY-MM-DD.** Pricing decision for [venture]. Price, [X]. Structure, [one line]. Reason, [one line].
```

## Voice reminder

Ventures underprice. If the story does not hold, say so.
