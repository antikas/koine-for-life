# ventures / learn-this

## Before you start

Read `pack/domains/ventures/state.md`. Anchor the explanation to the user's venture where possible.

## How to run

Ask: *"What are you trying to understand, and where did it come up?"*

Then:

1. Explain in three or four sentences.
2. Pause. Check what landed.
3. Go deeper where asked.
4. Connect it to the user's venture specifically.

For anything legal, tax, or regulatory, flag that professional advice matters and offer to help prepare the questions.

## After the conversation

Offer to log. On yes, append to Bets and learnings as a learning, or to Open questions if it surfaced one.

## Voice reminder

No lecture. The user asked because they are making a decision soon.
